About KPP

Know Pakistan Program (KPP) is a one-week program for Pakistani youth to promote awareness on different facets of life in Pakistan. KPP provide a unique forum for Pakistani students & young professionals to promote national integration and provide an opportunity to youth of the country to share their experiences see beautiful land, historical places and culture of other parts of the country. We believe that all young people should have access to personal development opportunities so that they can fulfil their potential in the future.

Our KPP Benefits

Pakistani Youth is brimming with ambition, enthusiasms, dynamism and commitment. In order to promote national integration and provide an opportunity to youth of the country to share their experiences see beautiful land, visit to places of historical importance/Monuments, visit to a villages to better understand the typical village rural life, visits to some industries, exposure of Pakistani media, interaction with faculty and students at a prestigious University/College/Institutes, interaction with NGOs and organizations , meetings with senior leadership/officials in Pakistan and taking part in cultural nights program.

Program Content

If you are seeking adventure, want to lose yourself in nature's wonders, looking for some cultural stimulation or just want to intermingle with the incredibly warm and hospitable people of Pakistan. Than this program is for you.
